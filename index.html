Welcome2

MyFMS (‘My Fiscal Management Services’) is a web-based Fiscal Management Service available to clients who are approved for voucher services by a Regional Center. At this time, services primarily target clients funded for voucher transportation services by the San Diego Regional Center. Volunteer drivers log trips for client approval on the web interface. MyFMS reimburses the appropriate volunteer driver for travel expenses based on rates established by the San Diego Regional Center and approved by the California Department of Developmental Services.

About
Community Interface Services

For over 30 years Community Interface Services has served children and adults with developmental disabilities in San Diego County and beyond. The agency was founded on the principle that people with developmental disabilities are in the best position to determine how they want to live. We provide services to people with intellectual disabilities, cerebral palsy, autism, seizure disorder and other similar conditions.

In addition to Fiscal Management Services, we offer support by providing Community Living, Vocational, Social Support, Housing, and Respite Services In natural, inclusive settings such as a person’s home or worksite and other community locations and by respecting and encouraging individual choice in self/participant directed services. Department of Developmental Services.Community Interface Services is excited to provide MyFMS!
